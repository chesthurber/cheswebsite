Dr. Ches Thurber an Assistant Professor in the Department of Political Science at Northern Illinois University. His research and teaching focus on global conflict, peace, and security. His scholarship has been published or is forthcoming in *International Studies Quarterly*, the *Journal of Global Security Studies*, *Conflict Management and Peace Science*, and elsewhere. He previously held research appointments at the Chicago Project on Security and Terrorism at the University of Chicago and at the Harvard Kennedy School's Belfer Center for Science and International Affairs. He received his Ph.D. from The Fletcher School of Law and Diplomacy at Tufts University and his B.A. from Middlebury College.
